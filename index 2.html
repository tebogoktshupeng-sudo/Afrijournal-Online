Chombo Unveils Proposal for Gender-Based Violence Legislation

The Botswana Gender and Policy Network noted that the Bill could help fill gaps that have historically allowed many offenders to evade punishment.

The Ministry of Youth and Gender Affairs has also urged the public to share their input and recommendations before the draft Bill is submitted to Parliament next year.
